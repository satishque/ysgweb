About YSG

Your Support Genie (YSG) was founded to help start-ups, micro, and small businesses address the technology challenges that come with growth by delivering dependable IT, cybersecurity, and cloud services tailored to their needs. Our goal is to become a trusted technology partner that keeps systems secure, reliable, and efficient, allowing business owners to focus on running and growing their business with confidence. Our vision is to make quality IT accessible to every business, regardless of size, delivered through responsive support, practical solutions, and clear, honest advice.

YSG is built on an entrepreneur's drive and years of hands-on technical support experience gained by solving real business challenges. That experience shapes our approach—practical, accountable, and focused on building long-term relationships through dependable service, proactive guidance, and technology solutions that grow alongside our clients and partners. We also partner with established MSPs to extend capacity and deliver successful outcomes for shared clients.
